# ⚔️ Battle Council  
### Smarter Monster Hunts. Faster Decisions. Better Outcomes.

Battle Council is a mobile-first strategy tool designed for **Total Battle players** who want to stop guessing and start optimizing.

It transforms raw game data into clear, actionable attack plans—so you know exactly **what to send, how much, and what you’ll lose** before you attack.

---

## 🚀 What This Does (In Plain Terms)

You pick a monster squad.  
Battle Council tells you:

- Which creatures to use
- How many to send
- What happens at different boost levels
- How many you’re likely to lose

No spreadsheets. No guesswork. No wasted troops.

---

## 🧠 Why It’s Different

Most tools give you data.  
Battle Council gives you **decisions**.

- ⚡ Built for **real-time gameplay**
- 📱 Designed **mobile-first**
- 🎯 Focused on **monster hunting only** (not generic combat math)
- 🧩 Combines **health, strength, and bonuses** into one view

---

## 🐲 Core Features

### 🔍 Monster Squad Breakdown
- View full squad composition
- Total **health + strength**
- Bonus modifiers (Mel, Rng, Mtd, Fly)
- Quick-read visual indicators

---

### ⚔️ Attack Planner (Core Engine)
Instantly generates attack formations based on your level:

- Calculates **units required**
- Scales from:
  - Base → 1200%
- Accounts for:
  - First strike mechanics
  - Overkill scenarios
  - Partial losses

---

### 🧬 Creature Optimization
- Ranks best creatures automatically
- Displays:
  - Formation number
  - Type + bonuses
  - Base stats
- Switch between formations instantly

---

### 📊 Smart Loss Estimation

Battle Council doesn’t just say “you win”—it shows the cost:

- 🟢 **0 losses** → clean win
- 🔴 **✖ / ALL** → don’t send it
- ⚖️ **Partial losses** → calculated using real health pools

---

## 📱 Built for Mobile Gameplay

- Fast switching between options

## 🧱 How It Works (Simple Version)

Under the hood, Battle Council calculates:

- Total monster health + strength
- Creature effective strength (with bonuses)
- Required unit count
- Losses based on incoming damage

Then it surfaces only what matters:
👉 **Send this. Expect this. Avoid this.**
